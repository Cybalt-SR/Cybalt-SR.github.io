- Title of content should be put at the top line (very first line of file just like the above).
- Paragraphs can be put anywhere.
- If you want to put a person with their position/role as a subtext, follow the format below:

[Title]Name of Person

- This will put the text inside the "[]" as a special text below the name of the person.
- If you want to make a collapsible section with a specific ministry, follow the format below:

\TITLE OF SECTION
CONTENT
/

- DO NOT forget the "/" at the end or else the website will put everything inside the collapsible section.
